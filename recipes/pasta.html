Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti, fettuccine, or pasta of choice
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups milk (whole milk preferred)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • ½ tsp Italian seasoning or parsley (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a kick)

Recipes:

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Add the Cheese
  4. Combine Pasta + Sauce
  5. Serve
